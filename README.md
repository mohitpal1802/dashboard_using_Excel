Project Description

This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance by analyzing sales data across various dimensions such as occasions, categories, time, products, and locations.

It helps in identifying revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance, enabling data-driven decision-making for business growth.

Key Points of the Dashboard

Overall Performance Metrics

Total Orders: 1000

Total Revenue: ₹35,20,984

Average Order-Delivery Time: 5.53 days

Average Customer Spend: ₹3,520.98

Revenue Insights

By Occasions: Revenue distribution across events like Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, and Valentine’s Day.

By Category: Performance comparison of product categories such as Cakes, Colors, Mugs, Plants, Soft Toys, and Sweets.

By Month: Monthly sales trend showcasing seasonal peaks (e.g., March and September show higher sales).

By Hour: Order placement trends throughout the day to understand customer purchase behavior.

Product Performance

Highlights the Top 5 Products by Revenue (e.g., Deserunt Box, Dolores Gift, Harum Pack, etc.), helping to identify best-sellers.

Geographical Insights

Displays Top 10 Cities by Orders, showing where most sales are concentrated (e.g., Dhanbad, Imphal, Kayali).

Interactivity

Filters available for Order Date, Delivery Date, and Occasion to dynamically explore data.
